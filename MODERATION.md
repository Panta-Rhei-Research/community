# Moderation

The discussion space is public, but moderated.

Maintainers may:

- move discussions to a better category;
- convert an issue into a discussion or vice versa;
- close duplicates;
- lock non-constructive conversations;
- remove spam or abuse;
- ask for a more specific formulation before engaging a broad critique.

The goal is not to avoid criticism.

The goal is to keep criticism inspectable, specific, and useful.
