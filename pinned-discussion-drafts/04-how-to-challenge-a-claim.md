# How to Challenge a Claim

A strong challenge names:

- the exact claim;
- where it appears;
- the suspected failure point;
- whether the issue is formal, empirical, conceptual, source-related, or rhetorical;
- what would count as failure;
- what evidence would resolve the issue.

Start in Discussions when the challenge is conceptual or not yet a concrete edit.

Open an Issue when you can identify a concrete defect.

Open a Pull Request when you can propose a concrete correction.
